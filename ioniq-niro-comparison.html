Verdict for Weekend and Motorway Use

For a driving pattern focused on weekend use, holidays and primarily motorway/A-road journeys:

  • Hyundai Ioniq: Offers superior fuel economy and lower emissions, making it more efficient for longer motorway journeys. The larger boot space with seats folded provides good practicality for weekend trips, though it has a conventional low driving position.
  • Kia Niro: Provides a higher crossover-style driving position that many drivers prefer, with slightly reduced fuel economy. The crossover styling offers more versatility for weekend adventures, though the boot is slightly smaller than the Ioniq's.

The Ioniq scores higher overall due to its superior efficiency and emissions performance, but the Niro might be preferred by those wanting a higher driving position and SUV styling, especially for occasional off-road parking during countryside weekend trips.
